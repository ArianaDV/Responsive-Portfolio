About Me

Hello! My name is Ariana, I am 26 years old, and a Chicago native. I attended the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I got a bachelor's degree in Finance.

I'm currently employed at U.S. Bank in their Corporate Trust department as a CDO Portfolio Administrator. I have been there for almost 3 years.

My desire to learn how to code and the reason for my sign up to the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp stems from my interest in SQL. I was introduced to it via a CS class at U of I. At U.S. Bank we use SQL to develop a lot of the tools that we need to provide accurate and efficient reporting to our clients. I currently am the end user of most of those tools but I would love to be part of the team that helps create them and have a bigger impact on our department.
